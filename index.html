    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are our most commonly asked questions. If you have any other queries or need any further assistance, please do not hesitate to Contact Us. We’re here to help you make the most of your CongoVIP experience.

    Can anyone become a CongoVIP member?

    Membership eligibility to CongoVIP is contingent upon review by our dedicated team. Please be advised that our team retains the authority to assess and either approve or decline membership based on our established criteria.

    What benefits do members receive?

    CongoVIP members are granted exclusive deals and services in DR Congo and around the world. Members receive exclusive VIP services, Deals and Discounts all around town in every business sector, Exclusive Privileges, Complimentary Benefits, Personalized Assistance by our team, Exclusive Access to events and so much more!

    What are the methods of payments you accept for a membership?

    We accept a variety of secure payment methods, including major credit cards, debit cards and mobile payment options such as M-pesa, Orange Money and Airtel. Although cash isn’t a preferred method, everything is possible for our VIP members. Contact us and we will find a solution.

    Can I upgrade my membership tier?

    Yes, you can upgrade your membership tier at any time. Simply open your Congo VIP app, draw the menu bar, click on VIP, and select your desired tier.

    How do I promote my business on CongoVIP?

    Simply download our mobile app, click on Contact Us, choose the relevant inquiry option and share your query. Our team will promptly respond to your message.
